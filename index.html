Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredents:

  • 1 Spagehetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • 1/2 of pancetta bacon
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Procedures

  • Boiling the spaghetti
      • 1 Bring a pot of salted water to boil.
      2 Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente.
      3 Drain the pasta and reserve about 1/2 cup of paste water.
      4 Reture the paste into the pot.

  • Preparering the Sauce
      • 1 In a bowl, whisk the eggs and mix in the parmesan cheese.
      2 Add a generous amount of freshly ground black peper.
      3 Mix well.
      4 In a Large skilet, heat the olive oil over medium heaat.
      5 Add the pancetta and cook until crispy.

  • Combining the pasta and the sauce
      • 1 Add the paste to the skillet with the pancetta.
      2 Quickly mix to coat the pasta in the fat
      3 Slowly pour the egg and cheese mixture while stirring quickly.
      the residual heat wil cook the eggs into a creamy sauce without scrambling them.
      4 Add some of the reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed.(optional)
  • Serve
      • 1 Plate the pasta and sprinkle extra parmesan and black pepper on top.
      2 Serve immediately.
